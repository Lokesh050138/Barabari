Mission Statement 2024

We are a section 8 Tech & Design Upskilling non-profit working towards:

  1. 1. Creating 500+ freelancing opportunities for students from rural low-income families.
  2. 2. Mentoring 500+ students from marginalized sectors employed in high-quality tech sector roles currently accessible to select universities.
  3. 3. Supporting 100+ affordable schools to set up computer education infrastructure and complete our digital literacy modules.
  4. 4. Funding 10+ candidates from marginalized communities to set up their tech entrepreneurial ventures.

Barabari Rozgaar Program

1. Candidates from marginalized communities get mentored esteemed tech professional toward their first tech job/intership

2. We help candidates build a strong tech foundation, build profecient communication skills, create an elaborate work profile and network with industry leaders.

3. Our core focus is on remote jobs and freelancing opportunites that help candidates from rural low-income families join the tech workforce smoothly

4. Working to make quality tech opportunites accessible to all

Barabari Labs

1. An afterschool learners and makers space to create first principles problem solvers

2. Enable candidates to develop design thinking, problem solving ability, understanding of industry relevant tools and communitcation skills.

3. Helping them to access paid industry projects leading to sustainable livelihoods.
